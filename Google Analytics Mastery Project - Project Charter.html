📋 1. Project Title & Description

Project Title: Google Analytics Mastery Project

This semester I will earn the Google Analytics certification while building an applied analytics project that demonstrates practical skills in tracking, analysis, and decision support. Deliverables include the certification credential, an applied analytics project using a dataset to answer a concrete business question, and a visual dashboard that demonstrates insights clearly.

🎯 2. Vision, Mission & Strategy Alignment

Personal Mission Connection:

My mission is to grow as a technology professional by using data and digital tools to solve problems, create efficiency, and make information more accessible to others. I am driven by curiosity, learning, and the desire to make a positive impact through my work.

Vision Alignment:

In 3–5 years, I see myself working as a data analyst or cybersecurity analyst, applying my skills in real-world settings to support organizations while continuing to expand my expertise. My vision is to build a career where I am constantly learning, contributing to meaningful projects, and setting the foundation for long-term growth in the tech industry.

Strategic Goal Support:

Supports my 12‑month goals to (1) complete an industry certification, (2) assemble a concise analytics portfolio, and (3) improve communication by writing clear, insight‑driven summaries.

💼 3. Business Case & Justification

Professional Value:

  • Adds a recognized industry credential (Google Analytics Certification) to my resume/LinkedIn.
  • Strengthens my analytics and reporting skill set (KPIs, dashboards, interpreting digital behavior).
  • Produces a portfolio artifact (analytics project + write-up) I can show to employers.
  • Improves my ability to discuss real-world data scenarios during interviews.

Personal Value:

  • Builds confidence in working with digital data and platforms.
  • Provides a structured and measurable milestone.
  • Offers personal satisfaction of achieving both a certification and a finished project in one semester.
  • Develops discipline through time management (balancing certification prep and project delivery).

Learning Outcomes:

  • Gain hands-on practice with web analytics concepts: metrics, dimensions, user flows, campaign attribution.
  • Improve ability to communicate insights visually and in writing.
  • Strengthen project management skills by planning and tracking deliverables.
  • Develop the ability to connect certification prep with applied projects: a transferable skill for future certifications.

📏 4. High-Level Scope

✅ In Scope for Semester Project

  • Complete Google Analytics Certification preparation (study modules, practice tests).
  • Earn the Google Analytics Certification (or provide exam confirmation if results are pending).
  • Develop an applied analytics project using a dataset and produce a case study report explaining methods, findings, and insights.
  • Create a visual dashboard (e.g., Tableau, Excel, or Google Data Studio) to showcase results.
  • Compile deliverables into a basic professional portfolio page or repository.

🗺️ Roadmap Items (≤6 Months)

  • Add a second dataset analysis to demonstrate broader concepts.
  • Build one additional visualization project in a different tool to expand skills.
  • Enhance the portfolio site with certification badges and improved design.
  • Pursue a related certification (e.g., Google Data Analytics or AWS Cloud Practitioner).
  • Share portfolio and certification on LinkedIn and GitHub to build visibility.

❌ Out of Scope

  • Pursuing multiple advanced certifications beyond Google Analytics.
  • Developing a full commercial analytics product or enterprise platform.
  • Creating multiple portfolio projects outside the defined applied project.
  • Ongoing long-term maintenance of the portfolio site.
